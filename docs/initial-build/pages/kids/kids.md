## Primary Message

Our Heavenly Father has made every child special and in His image and he loves them dearly -- and so do we. At Ev we partner with parents and caregivers to see children across Auckland share this vision.

Ev Kids provides a safe, caring environment so that you can focus on the service.

## Programs

### Preschool (Ages 1-4)

Female-led staff deliver Bible stories about Jesus, singing, and craft activities for children to take home.

### Primary (Ages 5-9)

Singing, games, and Bible memorization in peer groups. Messaging is coordinated with the adult service so families can discuss what they learned together.

### Fusion (School Years 6-8)

Deep Scripture study preparing children for their teen years as mature disciples.

## FAQ

### How does check-in work?

There is a registration table at the entrance with friendly staff to help you check in your children.

### What about first-time visitors?

Join the service and during Kids Talk the program transition will be explained. Staff are available to help ease children into the program.

### What are the staff qualifications?

Our leaders are people who love Jesus and share Him with kids at a level that they can understand and engage with. They are all New Zealand Police Vetted and Safe Ministry Trained. Staff prioritize a safeguarding culture with training to identify and mitigate risks.
