## Banner Image

- **Image**: Newish Connect banner

## Description

Newish Connect is for anyone who's new or newish to church and interested in finding out more about Auckland Ev. Over the course of a few weeks together, you'll hear how we started, where we are going, and how you can be involved.

Participants explore biblical content aligned with the church's core purposes, ask questions, and meet others.
