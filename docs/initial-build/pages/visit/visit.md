# What to Expect - Visit Us

## Campus Information

| Campus | Service Time | Address |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| North | Sunday 10:15am | 9-11 Rothwell Ave, Rosedale |
| Central | Sunday 10:15am | 80 Olsen Ave, Hillsborough |
| Unichurch | Sunday 5:15pm | Old Government House B102, Auckland Central |

## What to Expect

**Service Duration:** Approximately 90 minutes

**Service Structure:**

1. Music (worship)
2. Announcements
3. Kids dismissal (morning services)
4. Bible reading
5. Pastoral message
6. Closing worship

**Tone:** Exciting, casual, and relaxed

**Culture:** Come as you are. Ev Church welcomes everyone regardless of background or where they are on their faith journey.

**After the Service:** Following the service, there is time for fellowship with refreshments. This is a great opportunity to meet people and ask questions.

## Kids Ministry

Kids ministry is available at morning services for children from preschool through intermediate age.

**Check-In:** Children's check-in requires an extra 10 minutes before the service begins. First-time families should plan to arrive early to complete the registration process.

**Safety:** Ev Church maintains safety protocols for all kids programs, ensuring a secure environment for children.

> Note: Kids ministry is available at the North and Central campuses during the 10:15am morning services. Check with Unichurch regarding kids program availability at the evening service.
