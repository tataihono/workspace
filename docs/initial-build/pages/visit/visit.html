What to Expect

Our services run for approximately 90 minutes and the atmosphere is exciting, casual, and relaxed.

A Typical Service Includes

  1. Music (worship)
  2. Announcements
  3. Kids dismissal (morning services)
  4. Bible reading
  5. Pastoral message
  6. Closing worship

Come as you are. Ev Church welcomes everyone regardless of background or where you are on your faith journey.

After the service, stay for fellowship and refreshments. It's a great opportunity to meet people and ask questions.

Kids Ministry

Kids ministry is available at morning services for children from preschool through intermediate age.

Children's check-in requires an extra 10 minutes before the service begins. First-time families should plan to arrive early to complete the registration process.

Ev Church maintains safety protocols for all kids programs, ensuring a secure environment for your children.
