## Who We Are

Ev Youth targets students in school years 6 to 13, meeting Friday nights and Sunday mornings. The ministry fosters strong friendships and partnerships centred around exploring who Jesus is from the Bible in a safe community.

## Our Youth Team

Leaders are described as mature Christians who are captivated by Jesus. All leaders are police vetted and have completed a Safe Ministry Training course.

## Programs

### Juniors (Grades 6-9)

- **When**: Friday Nights, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
- **Description**: A great place to get to know people and get to know God through his Word. Includes games, a Bible talk, and small group discussions.

### Seniors (Grades 10-13)

- **When**: Friday Nights, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
- **Description**: An opportunity for high schoolers to connect with other high schoolers and connect with God through his Word. Includes games, hanging out, and small group Bible study.
