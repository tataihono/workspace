Who We Are

Ev Youth targets students in school years 6 to 13, meeting Friday nights and Sunday mornings. We foster strong friendships and partnerships centred around exploring who Jesus is from the Bible in a safe community.

Our Youth Team

Our leaders are mature Christians who are captivated by Jesus. All of our leaders are police vetted and have completed a Safe Ministry Training course.
