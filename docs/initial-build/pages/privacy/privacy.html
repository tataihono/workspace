Privacy Policy

Introduction

The Auckland Evangelical Church is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. We believe in being transparent about how we collect, use, and store your data. Personal information is collected and used primarily for pastoral care purposes, ensuring we can effectively serve and support our church community.

Collection

By completing a connect card you have voluntarily provided us with your personal information, and consented to us collecting that information from you.

Additional personal information may be collected through:

  • Direct contact with pastors and church leaders
  • Social media interactions, based on your privacy settings on those platforms

Purpose

Your personal information is used for:

  • Pastoral care and support
  • Keeping you informed about church events and activities

Data is stored on Rock RMS (Church Management System), which is hosted on church-managed servers.

Intended Recipients

Access to your personal information is restricted to:

  • Church staff
  • Executive members
  • Connect group leaders
  • Service team members

Your personal information will not be disclosed to any external parties without your consent, unless required by law.

Access

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Modify or update your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data

If you have any complaints regarding how your data is being treated, please contact us through our contact page.
