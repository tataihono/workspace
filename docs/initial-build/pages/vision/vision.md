# Our Vision

## Vision 2030

A bold vision for Aotearoa, New Zealand.

Our vision is to see lives changed as God grows His kingdom across Auckland and New Zealand by 2030.

---

## Mission Commitments

### Captivated by Christ

Magnifying God's glory in all that we do.

### Grounded in the Gospel

Authentic connection and sharing the good news of Jesus with the lost.

### Growing in Maturity and Number

Equipping gospel workers and planting churches throughout Auckland and New Zealand.

---

## Our History

- **2012**: Ev Church founded with 8 people.
- **February 2013**: First public service held at St Lukes Mall.
- **March 2014**: Unichurch launched on the University of Auckland campus.
- **February 2019**: Vision 2030 launched with 300+ members.
- **2021**: Central campus established at 80 Olsen Ave, Hillsborough. Third campus launched at Albany.
- **2025**: New north campus at 9-11 Rothwell Ave, Rosedale -- Ev Church's first purchased building.
- **Current**: 700+ members across three campuses.

---

## The Vision Goal

Our primary goal is to see 120,000 additional Aucklanders come to faith in Christ by 2030. Currently, only 2-3% of Auckland identifies as actively Christian, representing roughly 30,000 believers. We are praying and working to see that number rise to 150,000 -- lifting city-wide belief to 10%.

### Biblical Foundation

> "But we preach Christ crucified: a stumbling block to Jews and foolishness to Gentiles, but to those whom God has called, both Jews and Greeks, Christ the power of God and the wisdom of God."
> -- 1 Corinthians 1:23-24

The number 120,000 is drawn from the story of Jonah and Nineveh, where God showed compassion on a city of more than 120,000 people. We believe God has the same heart for Auckland.

---

## Four Strategic Objectives for 2030

1. **Grow to 2,030 members** across 6 campuses.
2. **Plant 4 Auckland and 4 New Zealand campuses** to extend gospel reach across the country.
3. **Train and send 30 people** into full-time gospel ministry.
4. **Establish a gospel training hub** to equip and resource church planters and ministry workers.
