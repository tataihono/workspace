## Description

Connect groups are smaller groups of Christians who meet midweek to help each other live as disciples of Jesus. We do this by spending time in God's word together unpacking what it means for our lives, by praying for and with each other, and by sharing our lives together in community.

## Newish Connect

Newish Connect runs for 4 weeks and functions as the first connect group for every Christian who calls Ev Church home.
