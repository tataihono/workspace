Services at Ev Church are all about Jesus. We gather each Sunday across our campuses to worship, hear from God's word, and encourage one another as a church family. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or have been following Jesus for years, you're welcome to join us. Our services typically run for about 90 minutes and include contemporary worship music, biblical teaching, and opportunities to connect with others.
